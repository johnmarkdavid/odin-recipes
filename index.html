Our History

Founded in 1997 as CookieRecipe.com, Odin Recipes changed the food world by providing the tools to share recipes and cooking tips, while celebrating the expertise of home cooks online. Since then, Odin Recipes has become the world's largest community-driven food brand, providing trusted resources to more than 60 million home cooks each month.
